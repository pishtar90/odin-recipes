Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that combines layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese filling. It's a classic Italian dish that is loved by many. The flavors are rich and comforting, making it the perfect comfort food. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or just want to enjoy a homemade meal, this lasagna recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned and the onion is translucent. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a separate pot, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
  6. Arrange a single layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spoon a layer of the meat sauce over the noodles.
  8. Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  9. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sauce and cheese on top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
