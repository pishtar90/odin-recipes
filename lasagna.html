Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that combines layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese filling. It's a classic Italian dish that is loved by many. The flavors are rich and comforting, making it the perfect comfort food. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or just want to enjoy a homemade meal, this lasagna recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
